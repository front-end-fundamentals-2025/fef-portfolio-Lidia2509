This is me

My name is Lidia Pawlos Ghebre. I am 21 years old and currently studying New Media Design at Jönköping University. I am of Eritrean origin but was born and raised in Bologna, Italy. I enjoy photography, cooking, and traveling. I am passionate about creativity and love engaging in activities that allow me to express it. Some of the hobbies I have developed because of this passion include crocheting, photography, drawing, and painting.
